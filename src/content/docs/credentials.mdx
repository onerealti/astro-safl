## Project Development Timeline (Appendix I)

The project followed a structured lifecycle from concept and requirement analysis to field testing:

<Steps>

1. **Phase 1: Problem Identification & Setup (November 2024)**
   * *Activities*: Field visits, literature review, and engineering requirement gathering.
   * *Deliverables*: Project objective definition and preliminary report submission.

2. **Phase 2: Conceptual CAD Modeling (December 2024)**
   * *Activities*: Conceptual drafting, part-by-part SolidWorks modeling of chassis and leg links.
   * *Deliverables*: Initial mechanical design layouts and 3D models.

3. **Phase 3: Structural Validation & FEA (January 2025)**
   * *Activities*: Stress, strain, and deflection simulations on SolidWorks; design feedback loop.
   * *Deliverables*: FEA simulation report and optimized CAD model.

4. **Phase 4: Mechanical Fabrication (February 2025)**
   * *Activities*: Laser-cutting 3mm steel plates, FDM 3D printing of PETG shells and brackets.
   * *Deliverables*: Fully assembled physical robot frame structure.

5. **Phase 5: Hardware Integration (March 2025)**
   * *Activities*: Mounting NVIDIA Jetson Nano and sensors; wiring layout and vibration isolators setup.
   * *Deliverables*: Integrated electrical and computing platform on chassis base.

6. **Phase 6: Edge ML Deployment (April 2025)**
   * *Activities*: Edge CNN weed classification setup on Jetson Nano, serial telemetry synchronization.
   * *Deliverables*: Calibrated multi-sensor bot running local real-time inference.

7. **Phase 7: Field Testing & Thesis Binding (May 2025)**
   * *Activities*: Loaded field traversal trials, thermal measurements log, results recording, and thesis binding.
   * *Deliverables*: Performance validation reports and final bound dissertation.

</Steps>

## Relevance to Environment, Safety, & Ethics (Appendix II)

The project contributions were evaluated against the standard National Board of Accreditation (NBA) **Program Outcomes (PO1 to PO12)**:

*(Mapping scale: 3 = Highly Relevant, 2 = Moderately Relevant, 1 = Less Relevant)*

    *   *Contribution*: Applied mechanical design, electronics integration, and edge ML principles to solve real-world agricultural automation challenges.
*   **PO2: Problem Analysis (Mapping: 3)**
    *   *Contribution*: Analyzed field requirements and translated them into specific mechanical, electrical, and software specifications.
*   **PO3: Design/Development of Solutions (Mapping: 3)**
    *   *Contribution*: Engineered a modular, stable, and terrain-adaptable quadruped wheeled robot chassis addressing specific farming needs.
*   **PO4: Conduct Investigations of Complex Problems (Mapping: 2)**
    *   *Contribution*: Performed FEA-based structural stress/deflection simulations and validated them against physical field data.
