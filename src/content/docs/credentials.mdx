## Project Development Timeline (Appendix I)

The project followed a structured lifecycle from concept and requirement analysis to field testing:

### Phase 1: Mechanical Design & Chassis Fabrication (Nov 2024 – May 2025)

<Steps>

1. **Stage A: Requirements Setup (November 2024)**: Problem identification, literature review, field interviews, and project parameter definition.
2. **Stage B: CAD Modeling & Mechanical Simulation (December 2024 – January 2025)**: SolidWorks design of the wheeled-leg assemblies and Finite Element Analysis (FEA) for stress and deflection.
3. **Stage C: Frame Fabrication & Assembly (February 2025 – May 2025)**: Steel plate laser cutting, chassis welding, and FDM 3D printing of custom brackets and component enclosures.

</Steps>

### Phase 2: Hardware Integration, Prototyping & Field Validation (Sept 2025 – May 2026)

<Steps>

1. **Stage A: Hardware & ML Integration (September 2025 – October 2025)**: Sensor harness routing, Jetson Nano setup, power distribution wiring, and local CNN classification model deployment.
2. **Stage B: Field Testing & Academic Submission (November 2025)**: Initial terrain traversal verification, compilation of final project report, and successful academic review board presentation.
3. **Stage C: System Ruggedization & Optimization (December 2025 – February 2026)**: Improving wiring harness layout, installing physical vibration isolators, upgrading voltage regulation, calibrating soil probe telemetry, and expanding regional weed datasets.
4. **Stage D: Traversal Testing & Navigation Control (March 2026 – April 2026)**: Testing semi-autonomous traversal on uneven agricultural soil and verifying manual controller overrides.
5. **Stage E: Competition Validation & Award (May 2026)**: Live field validation trials at the city-wide **MAKEFORHYDERABAD** Make-a-thon, winning **1st Place** (Cash Prize ₹10,000) for a field-ready agricultural rover.

</Steps>
