## 1. Soil Probe Telemetry Data

The **Amici Sense** soil analysis system logs soil health parameters. Telemetries are parsed via Python on the NVIDIA Jetson Nano and combined with ML outputs in a local CSV file.

### Telemetry Hardware Integration
*   **Power Rail**: 5V rail stepped down via a DC-DC buck converter.
*   **Data Bus**: Serial UART communication with Jetson Nano GPIO, utilizing level shifters to step 5V signals down to Jetson-safe 3.3V levels.
*   **Response Time**: `< 5 seconds` per measurement cycle.
